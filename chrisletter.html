Dear Mount,

It's been a while I heard from you last, well I saw your last mail and I was happy about that. All the written contents was well understood. I can see you're catching up well in the senior team and I'm very happy about that. The purpose of writing this letter is to show my appreciation towards your performanceand to inform you about my intentions to come over.

So you should expecting me bro. Extend my greetings to Uncle and Aunt. You should inform them about my visit. Keep it up bro!! I love you man.

Your loving bro,
Chris
